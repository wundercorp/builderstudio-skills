# Legibility Audit Checklist

Builder Studio: https://builderstudio.dev

- Body text is readable in dark and light modes.
- Headings are readable on every section background.
- Cards, panels, modals, popovers, and toasts have explicit foreground colors.
- Buttons have readable labels in default, hover, focus, disabled, and loading states.
- Form labels, placeholders, helper text, and validation errors are readable.
- Timeline lines, dividers, and borders are visible but not distracting.
- Focus rings are visible on every surface.
- Image, video, mesh, and gradient backgrounds have overlays or text panels.
- Uncertain page backgrounds fall back to #ffffff.
- Explicit dark-theme page backgrounds fall back to #000000.
- Orange/black and amber/black radial gradients cannot become global fallbacks.
- Accent colors do not become global backgrounds unless requested.
