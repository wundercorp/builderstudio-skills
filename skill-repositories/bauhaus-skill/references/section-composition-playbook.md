# Section Composition Playbook

Builder Studio: https://builderstudio.dev

## Hero

- one oversized headline
- one focal object or visual
- one accent framing device such as a ring, panel, or bar
- one primary CTA and one secondary action at most

## Product showcase

- strong product title
- concise supporting paragraph
- thumbnail or variant strip
- section background that contrasts with the hero

## Feature section

- two to four compact feature points
- bold numbers or short labels
- background inversion or accent band when needed

## Quote or story section

- calmer typography scale
- more whitespace
- let the accent color show up in small details only

## Footer

- return to high contrast
- keep the palette consistent
- do not introduce a new color family
